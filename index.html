📝 Legitimate vs Fraudulent Offer Letter

✅ Legitimate Signals
• Official company domain email (@company.com)
• CIN / GST / PAN of company mentioned
• HR name, designation, phone
• Fixed salary with payslip structure
• Clear joining date & probation terms
• No fee, deposit or advance payment
• Physical office address
• Interview process clearly stated
• E-signed with digital certificate
❌ Fraudulent Signals
• Gmail / Yahoo / Hotmail email
• No company registration details
• "Pay ₹X fee to confirm joining"
• No salary figure or vague CTC
• Contact only via WhatsApp
• "100% selection guaranteed"
• Urgency: "reply in 24 hours"
• Send PAN / Aadhaar photo
• No office address listed

  • ❌

  • 🏛
    File police FIR at local station with Cyber Crime reference number
  • 📢
    Report the UPI/phone number on NPCI Grievance Portal and TRAI DND portal
